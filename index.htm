Newcastle Libraries open data

@ToonLibraries are opening up as much of their data as possible, under a Creative Common Public Domain licence. This means they have waived all rights to the data, you can use it however you wish.

Usage data is detailed by month and branch since 2008. Issues are monthly figures of items being lent from the catalogue. Visits are the number of people entering a library. Enquiries are any question asked of library staff by a member of the public. PC usage is given as a percentage of the total available time computers can be booked.
