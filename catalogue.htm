@ToonLibraries are opening up their data, under a Creative Commons Public Domain licence.

Catalogue data is made up of Items and Title datasets. Titles are the list of titles in the catalogue, including title, ISBN, price, author and publisher. Items are individual items held, all of which are linked to a Title record, but also have a date added to catalogue, library they're held at, and a count of issues and renewals.
