@ToonLibraries are opening up their data, under a Creative Commons Public Domain licence.

Member data lists each current library member, including their postcode district (e.g. NE1), the library they are registered at, the date added to represent the date they joined, as well as time added for the time they joined. A last used date and last used time also show the date and time of the last activity by the user.
